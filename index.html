About Akruthi Sarees

Founded in 2024, Akruthi Sarees is a celebration of India's rich textile heritage. We curate exquisite handcrafted sarees from across the country, bringing you authentic weaves with contemporary elegance.

Our name 'Akruthi' means 'form' or 'expression' in Sanskrit, representing how each saree in our collection is a unique expression of artistry and tradition.

Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We collect personal information when you make a purchase, create an account, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details. We use this information solely to process your orders and enhance your shopping experience.

Data Security

Your personal information is protected through SSL encryption on our website. We implement industry-standard security measures and never store your complete payment details. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

Communication

We may use your contact information to send order updates, respond to inquiries, and occasionally share promotions. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link provided in each email.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve functionality and analyze site traffic. These help us understand customer preferences and enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. For any privacy-related requests, please contact us at privacy@akruthisarees.com. We may update this policy periodically, with changes posted on this page.
